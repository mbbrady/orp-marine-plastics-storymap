Chesapeake Bay Microplastic Survey

The Ocean Research Project conducted a comprehensive microplastic survey of the Chesapeake Bay in Fall 2023 aboard the SRV Marie Tharp. Our team collected samples from 25 stations across the bay, analyzing microplastic concentrations, water quality parameters, and environmental conditions.
